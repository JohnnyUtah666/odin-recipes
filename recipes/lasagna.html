Lasagna

The Most Bangin' Lasagna on Both Sides of the Mississippi

You're going to be missing out big time if you don't dig into this delectable piece of Italian-American history. Just follow the recipe below to take an express bus to Flavor Town!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 (32 oz) Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Spread out your noodles
  2. Slap those layers together with pride, sauce first
  3. Stick it in the oven at 350 for about 35 minutes
