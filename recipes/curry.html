Air-Fryer Crispy Curry Drumsticks

These are crispy drumsticks that can be produced with an air-fryer

You already know these are going to taste real nice, so we won't hold you any longer; read on for the recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken in large bowl; add salt and enough water to cover
  2. Toss chicken in air-fryer at 375 for 15-17 minutes, remove and add remaining seasonings
